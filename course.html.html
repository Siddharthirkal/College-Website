Bachelors's in Computer Application
[BCA]

The BCA course is a full time three years (six semesters) Bachelor's Degree in Computer Application. The basic objective of BCA Course is to provide young men and women with the required knowledge and necessary skills to get rewarding careers into the changing world of Information Technology.

There are three years of study required for an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Information Technology. An undergraduate program in computer applications is offered at the university. Following the completion of class 12, candidates are eligible to apply for the BCA program. There's no doubt that the BCA course is most popular among students majoring in computer science. This is because it prepares them for demonstrating their skills in the IT industry through internships.

The program is open to BCA graduates with a minimum average of 50% and a minimum of 3 years experience. There is no distinction between scientists and non-scientists when it comes to applying for the BCA course.
